Gombot is an app that securely remembers your passwords and lets you sign in to your favorite websites with just one click.

Gombot even helps you take your passwords with you; it will be available on Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome and Apple iPhone/iPod Touch.

Now there's no need to remember so many passwords anymore. Just type in a single master password and Gombot does the rest for you. For even greater security, Gombot can PIN protect your stored password before signing you in to a site. That's extra important when you want that extra layer of safety for your personal data when using email, Facebook or online banking.

Gombot is made by Mozilla, the global non-profit organization that answers to no one but you.
